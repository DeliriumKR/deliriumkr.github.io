## Next Session Intro
---
After finding Ireena atop Lugdana's Knoll, the party bid a heartfell farewell to Ismark and finally set off from the Village of Barovia for Tser pool to the North-west; their goal there was to meet with the Seer, Madame Eva, and discern a way to defeat Strahd and free themselves from his realm.

While on the road, the party decided to check out Van Richten's Weapons Cache that Doru had told them about, but found it surrounded by the undead barovian family, turned in the seige, the Lanstens. With a command, Sir Gus sent the corpse of the mother home to her village, her children following behind. The group then decided to pacify the Father via combat. Following this, they retrieved the weapon's cache and returned with it and the corpse to the road.

Leaving the Father's corpse on the road, the party made their way further on, eventually reaching the River Ivlis Crossroads. There, they met with Count Strahd von Zarovich, lord and ruler of Barovia, for the first time. Strahd admitted his interest in the party, and posed questions regarding their motivations while revelling in their uncertainty and apprehension. Eventually, he humoured their reasoning, conceding his points if only to humour them.
Lastly, as a parting gift Strahd gave back to Sir Gus his shield, coated in blood and weighed down with the severed arm of his missing companion. Strahd then departed, heading Southbound at the crossroads.

The party continued, reaching a clearing in the forest. There, a desperate squawk broke their silence and a familiar Raven with blue tipped wings emerged from the bushes, fleeing from a swarm of strange wicker constructs led by a large horrifying creature of twigs and magic.
The party rallied to arms, fighting the horrors and emerging victorious.

After brief recovery, the party continued on, eventually reaching their destination of Tser pool, just as the evening light touches the skies. As the joyous music and river trickle fills your ears, you approach the encampment, and a figure approaches you with a wide smile.

"Welcome, welcome! You seem wary from travel friends, come warm yourself by the fires if you need, there's plenty of space to go around. Madame Eva mentioned that she was expecting you." His warm smile brightens the din, and as far as you can tell he seems genuinely pleased to welcome guests. The stage returns to you, and you find yourselves in character once more.

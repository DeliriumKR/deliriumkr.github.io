Located to the West, Veyr plays a crucial role in the [[Kingdom of Eiraya]] thanks to its Rich Farmlands and idyllic location


Geography: Rolling plains, vast farmland, gentle rivers—Eiraya’s granary.

Culture: Peaceful, industrious, highly devout. Seen as the “soul” of the realm.

Seat: Haldrith Vale

Status: Fell early. Its churches were the first to convert to Caeren’s flame cult.

Position: Loyal to Caeren, whether by belief or survival.

Relations:

Friendly with Caulderyn (military protection).

Quietly resented by Nareth and Velryn.

Motives: Seeking peace and food security. Would prefer neutrality, but sees submission as the safer road.
